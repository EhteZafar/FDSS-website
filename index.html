About Us

The modern world is all about data. We have more than 100 zettabytes of data within the world, and it is increasing every minute. This data needs to be stored, managed, manipulated, and used to our benefit. Data Science is one of the highest-growing fields in the industry. The FAST Data Science Society aims to introduce every student to the world of Data Science who is passionate and wishes to learn more about it. Our Society provides a platform to answer all students’ questions and solve their queries. We wish to inspire and excite everyone about Data Science through presentations, workshops, and research. Our utmost aim is to bridge the gap between students and industry by introducing them to successful, experienced, and highly talented professionals who have worked in the industry so that they can share their success stories and guidance with the future generation.
